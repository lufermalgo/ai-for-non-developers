# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We respect people of all backgrounds and welcome everyone—regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Insulting or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Any other conduct that would be reasonably considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this code of conduct may be asked to stop their behavior or may be removed from community spaces at the discretion of maintainers.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing [fmaldonado@summan.com](mailto:fmaldonado@summan.com) with:
- A description of what happened
- Who was involved
- Any relevant context

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.
